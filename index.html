About

Consilience

Consilience is an annual flagship Conference hosted by the Law and Technology Society of NLSIU. Every year, the focus is on a topic of utmost contemporary relevance regarding the interface between law and technology. Since the first edition of the Conference, Consilience has been an endeavour to explore the depths of the selected theme, deliberate on the issues involved and clarify the ambiguities. It is unparalleled in terms of the tremendous legal acumen that is witnessed everyday in the Conference. The determination to promote understanding of the chosen topic is demonstrated by the submission of a recommendatory report, which critically analyses the discussions amongst esteemed academicians and professionals in the relevant field to arrive to legally sound and practically viable suggestions to resolve the controversies. With its unique amalgam of law and technology, Consilience serves as a fertile platform for encouraging both creativity and practical solutions.

National Law School of India University

National Law School of India University or NLSIU, Bangalore is widely- recognized as India’s premier law institute. The University was established in 1986 to cater to the requirements of the legal profession, including teaching, research and judiciary. It signified a culmination of the combined efforts of the Judiciary, the Bar Council of India, the Karnataka Bar Council, the Bangalore University and the Government of Karnataka. The Chief Justice of India serves as the Chancellor of the University. Today NLSIU is synonymous with unparalleled education and opportunities in the field of legal education. It offers a comprehensive education that includes undergraduate, post-graduate, research degrees, distant education and endowment chairs. Since inception, it has been a socially responsible institution organizing numerous workshops, seminars, competitions and conferences along with vibrant cultural and sporting events that lead to a holistic education of some of India’s potentially finest legal minds.
